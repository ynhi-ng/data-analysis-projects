Key Insights
verified against the cleaned dataset
  • Timing: orders rise Monday through Thursday, then drop — Thursday promotions and a midday (10am–3pm) flash-sale window line up with actual order volume.
  • Concentration: the UK generates ~83% of all revenue; Netherlands, EIRE, Germany and France are the next tier, each far smaller.
  • Segmentation: RFM scoring splits customers into 10 groups — Hibernating is the largest single group (1,067 customers), meaning re-engagement campaigns have real reach.
  • Recommendation: a hybrid content + co-purchase recommender can now surface both textually-similar and commonly co-bought products (see accompanying notebook code).
